Where is the centralized computer center located?

In the administrative block

Is the computer center available around the clock?

Yes, it is available 24/7

What is the first rule to be followed while using the library?

Write your name in the visitor's register every time.

What should visitors do with their ID-card when using the library?

Deposit your ID-card at the counter and collect it while leaving.

Where should visitors keep their bags and belongings while in the library?

Keep your bags and other belongings at the property counter near the entrance.

What is prohibited in the stack area of the library?

Do not carry any personal or issued books in the stack area.

Before entering the stack room, what should visitors do?

Seek permission of the library staff.

What are the expectations regarding noise and discipline in the reading hall?

Maintain absolute silence and strict discipline in the reading hall. Switch off your mobiles.

Is carrying eatables allowed in the library?

Do not carry eatables in the library.

What should visitors do in response to instructions from the librarian or staff at the counter?

Follow instructions given by the librarian or the staff at the counter.

How many books are issued to UG/PG students and SC/ST students, respectively?

Three books to UG/PG students and two books to SC/ST students (per two weeks) will be issued against their ID-Card.

What should users do at the time of issuing a book?

Check the book at the time of issuing. In case of any defect or damage please inform the library staff.

What is the deadline for returning an issued book?

Return the issued book on or before the due date.

What is the consequence of not renewing a book before the due date?

Renewal is compulsory before the due date (within 15 days from the issued date).

Which types of materials are not issued outside the library?

Books marked "REFERENCE" will not be issued outside the library.

What is the user's responsibility in case of a lost or damaged book?

Replacement of the book has to be made by the user in case of lost or damage of a book.
